"Jewels, Gems, and Halflings"

Town: Baggister

Dell, the female halfling jeweler's shopkeep was robbed and she lost all her gems. Stolen by Tetaris, the location of the missing loot is in an old barnhouse outside of town. Return the stolen gems.

"A Baker's Dozen"

Town: Baggister

Bira, the barkeep at the Lusty Goat said Nathan the baker was killed outside of town by orcs, which is why his shop has been closed for a few days, and she's worried for her own safety. Investigate the disappearance.

"Geda's Ghouls"

Town: Baggister

Ermina the blacksmith said an old friend of her's talked about flesh eating ghouls lurking in the ruins of Geda's Hold just outside town. Her friend was passing through town to buy equipment but she hasn't heard from him in a few days and is a bit worried. Look into it for her, she's offered a discount in return. 25% off anything in her shop.

"A Halfling's Other Half"

Town: Baggister

Dell, the halfling jeweler at the 'Unicorn's Choker' was so happy to have her gems back, but most of all a flawless diamond she said was worth 'a life' and admittedly kept something back. Dell aims to bring back a loved one who's recently died, but she cannot recover the body nor perform the ritual. She need's a high cleric AND the body of the deceased.

"Local Legends"

Town: Baggister

Brak the Goblin Rogue, from the Order of the Fang mentioned he was on a mission to find a half-orc named Luko, when he ran into the baker and his wolf accidentally killed him. Brak owe's the party a favor and has agreed to cut everyone in on the loot whenever the mission is completed. The problem is he doesn't know what he's supposed to do with this 'Luko', help Brak out.

"Tipping the Scales"

Town: Baggister

After losing much gold in an ambush at the Snake Pit arena, the party rushes to get it back. Prepared for adventure they wonder where to start, when a message is delivered to them. It reads:

'It has come to my attention that my goblin idiot has taken your gold, but also that you have killed my assassins and prevented them from completing the mission I had given them. Impressive, but do not be too proud, it was a team meant for just one target. I'll offer you a deal. Bring the one called Luko to me, and I will return your gold. I do look forward to meeting you all in person.'

A map is included with the message.
